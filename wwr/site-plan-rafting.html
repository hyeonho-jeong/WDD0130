Overview

Purpose

Jeong’s Water Rafting is one of the best rafting places in the world. Every year more than a million people visit our water rafting place. We haven’t had a single casualty in 10 years. Before rafting, all passengers are thoroughly trained for warm-up and safety, and safety personnels are on standby for emergencies. So don’t worry and just enjoy Jeong’s water rafting!

Audience

If You want to make wonderful memories with your family or friends? If you are scared because you’ve never done water rafting before? If you worry about your finances because a lot of people are trying to enjoy it together? If so, don’t worry, and just come to Jeong’s Water Rafting! We have 7 different courses to do water rafting. Depending on your level, you can choose the courses. And if you visit Jeong’s water rafting with more than 15 people, we will give 15% discounts on the total price.
