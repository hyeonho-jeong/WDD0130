Jeong's Art Gallery runs an art museum education where you can discover the dynamics, experimentation, and value of art in your daily life. The educational program, with participants from various cultural backgrounds from infants to adults, aims to provide visitors with meaningful learning to experience modern art and communicate with society through art, and an open art museum for everyone.

Children

We develop and operate experience exhibitions and various educational programs for children and families. In addition, we run a program in connection with art play spaces with the theme of art, nature, and play in outdoor spaces. We would like to help future generations explore the meaning of modern art and experience various art education, and create a "participating art museum, open art museum" for children and families.

Teenager

In order to expand the understanding of modern art and the value of art to future generations, we would like to contribute to fostering future convergence talents that grow with art as well as public education courses. It also recognizes and respects the differences of members of society based on the various attributes of modern art and helps them discover the value of cultural diversity.

Adults

For the purpose of understanding exhibitions, collections, art culture, and expanding cultural enjoyment rights, we are actively participating in various programs such as lectures, seminars, and workshops.

Senior

By participating in art lectures, self-esteem was formed and a consensus was formed by narrowing the difference between generations that may occur in the learning field through art education, learning experience, and aesthetic experience. In addition, the social development of the elderly is carried out through friendship outside of learning, which helps most elderly people suffering from loneliness and depression to recognize their social existence. . In addition, it is hoped that leisure programs for the elderly will develop various programs as true culture and arts that actually value the lives of the elderly.
